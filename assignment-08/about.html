About

I chose to give instructions on a paper airplane becuase I have been making them since I was little.
Making airplanes is something I have always done and people always comment on how cool the plane looks and how well it flies.
Usually people's first response is "Whoa how did you make that?" So this way I can show them.
I also wanted to do something simple and that didn't require many materials.
You can find out more by checking out a book on paper airplanes at the Missoula Public Libray!
