How to Make the Best Papaer Airplane

Materials

  • Piece of Paper (8.5 x 11)
  • Two Hands

Step 1

Fold the piece of paper in half, hotdog style.

Step 2

Fold each corner in to make a triangle.

Step 3

Fold the triangle down towards you.

Step 4

Fold the corners in at an angle like so.

Step 5

Fold the little triangle below where the folds met, up and secure the corners.

Step 6

Fold the whole paper in half along the fold from Step 1.

Step 7

Fold the wings down.

Step 8

You are finished! You made the worlds greatest airplane. Have fun!
